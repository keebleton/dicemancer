Drop sound files here and the game uses them automatically (the built-in
synth plays for any file that is missing). Formats: .mp3, .ogg, or .wav.

Expected names:

  dice          the 2d6 roll
  coin          money gained
  points        points gained
  heal          HP restored
  buy           a card purchase
  win           the victory sting

  damage_red    fire woosh
  damage_blue   splash
  damage_black  dark magic woosh
  damage_green  natural or wind magic
  damage_yellow light magic or light blade
  damage        generic fallback for colorless or starter card hits

The damage voice is picked by the color of the CARD that dealt the hit.
Keep clips short (under ~1.5 s) and roughly the same loudness.
